ABOUT GATE EXAMINATION

The Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) is an examination that primarily tests the comprehensive understanding of undergraduate subjects in engineering and science. The exam is conducted jointly by the Indian Institute of Science and seven Indian Institutes of Technologies at Roorkee, Delhi, Guwahati, Kanpur, Kharagpur, Chennai (Madras) and Mumbai (Bombay) on behalf of the National Coordination Board – GATE, Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Human Resources Development (MHRD), Government of India.


IMPORTANCE

GATE scores enable a student to gain admission into the Postgraduate Program at Indian higher education institutes, as well as jobs in various Public Sector Companies. GATE is known to be one of the most competitive exams in India. GATE scores not only hold value in India but also across various institutions across the globe.

About GATE – ECE Exam

GATE – ECE (Gratitude Aptitude Test in Engineering – Electronics and Communication Engineering) is a field of engineering which deals with the Network, Analog circuit, Digital circuit, Electronic devices, signal and system, control system, communication, electromagnet. This exam is conducted for the students who want to take admission in the electrical and communication engineering field. This exam is conducted for a fair chance of admission for all the students. Students who passed in GATE – ECE are offered admission letters from various universities. It is a national level entrance exam.

The Exam Pattern

• Engineering and Mathematical questions are compulsory and carry 15% weightage of the paper (15 marks)

• The questions based on subject of the exam carry 70% weightage of the paper (70 marks)

• The remaining part of GATE question paper includes General Aptitude Test it carries 15% weightage of paper (15 marks)

  • Maths and Engineering (No of Questions 15)
    (Duration in Mins 60)
  • General Aptitude (No of Questions 15)
    (Duration in Mins 60)
  • Section B to H (No of Questions 35)
    (Max. Marks 60)

Total Questions are 65 and The test total time is: 3 Hours

THE SCORE VALIDITY

The score validity of GATE – ECE marks is 4 years. Candidates can apply in universities with that particular score for four years. Once the validity expires, Candidates have to reappear for the examination to get new score for admission.

Exam Eligibility

Candidates applying for GATE – EE examination should have a Bachelor’s degree in Engineering/Architecture/Technology (4 years after 10+2 / Post B. Sc / Post – Diploma) and those who are in the final year of such programs are eligible to apply for GATE – EE.

Career Scope after Exam

As an ECE graduate, you can make a career in industries like manufacturing, consumer electronics manufacturing, digital telecommunications industry, IT, health care tools manufacturing, mobile communication sector, power electronics.

Some prominent Job titles in the domain are:

• Electronic engineer

• Electronic and communication engineer

• Electronic Design and development engineer

• Desktop system engineer

• System control engineer
