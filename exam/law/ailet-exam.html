A Brief Study About AILET Exam

The All India Law Entrance Test or AILET conducted by the National Law University or NLU Delhi allows the students to seek admission in various law courses at all levels. This entrance examination tests a candidate's legal aptitude and reasoning abilities. AILET is mainly for those candidates who want to pursue law degree courses from National Law University Delhi. Although NLU Delhi only accepts the examination as a qualifying entrance examination. Several other private law colleges in India grant admission to eligible candidates based on their respective AILET scores. Conducted only once a year through offline examination mode, it’s one of the most sought after entrance examinations for law degree courses in India.

The Exam Pattern of AILET

  • English (No of Questions 35)
  • General Knowledge (No of Questions 35)
  • Basic Mathematics (No of Questions 10)
  • Legal Aptitude (No of Questions 35)
  • Reasoning (No of Questions 35)

The test total time is: 90 Mins

The Exam Dates

NLU conducts the examination only once a year, and generally online applications start from January. The exam occurs around mid-year. The online application process for AILET 2020 has started from 15th January 2020, and they extended the last date up to 10th August 2020. The scheduled time for AILET 2020 was on 18th August 2020.

But due to the current Covid-19 situation, the scheduled date for the AILET 2020 examination has been postponed. The conducting body has not yet announced any alternative date for the exam.

THE SCORE VALIDITY

Since AILET is a college-specific entrance examination, the score validity of the exam remains immediate. Just like any institute specific admission test, AILET also grants admission to the qualifying candidates of the next academic year. Unlike CLAT and other national level entrance examinations, the candidates who have recently qualified AILET can take admission to NLU Delhi and some other private colleges that accept the examination. However, there is no upper age limit provided for application to this entrance test.

AILET Exam Eligibility

Since AILET grants admissions to UG, PG as well as doctoral law courses, the eligibility criteria under each level varies.

LLB: Any candidate who has completed 10+2 or equivalent examination from a recognized board with minimum aggregate marks of 50% is eligible to apply for AILET LLB. Class 12 students who are appearing in the board's examinations in the same academic year are also available.

LLM: Candidates must have an LLB or any equivalent law degree from a recognized college with a minimum score of 55% are eligible to apply for AILET LLM. LLB final semester students can also apply.

PhD: Candidates who have qualified LLM or equivalent degree from a recognized institute with minimum aggregate marks of 55% are eligible to apply for AILET PhD. https://www.shiksha.com/law/ailet-exam

Exam Fee

The application fees for AILET is differentiated based on categories. For AILET 2020, the application fees for candidates belonging to General/OBC/EWS categories is INR 3,050 only. At the same time, the application fees for candidates of SC/ST/PWD categories are INR 1,050 only. However, the application fees are an exemption for Below Poverty Line (BPL) candidates.

How to Apply for the AILET Exam?

For appearing in AILET, you need to undergo an online application process. The step by step online application procedure is given below.

Step 1: Visit the official website for AILET and click on the ‘Apply Online’ link.

Step 2: Fill all the required details asked for. It will include personal information like name, contact information, email id, address, date of birth, and guardians’ name.

Step 3: After filling the form with correct details, click on the Registration button.

Step 4: This will redirect the web page to the Application Locker Page and confirm the given details after adequately checking it.

Step 5: After the registration is complete, choose a college and click on the ‘Click Here to Apply’ button.

Step 6: On the next page, select the targeted course from the drop-down list.

Step 7: Then proceed on by clicking on the ‘Apply Now’ button.

Step 8: Fill the AILET application form in the redirected page with all the required details. The details must be original.

Step 9: Upload a recently clicked passport size photograph in the specified details and click on the ‘Save’ button to continue.

Step 10: Pay the stipulated amount of application fees as guided through online mode - via Credit Card/Debit Card or Net Banking. Once the online payment is made, the online application procedure is complete.

Benefits of taking the EXAM

AILET is one of the most demanded entrance examinations for admission to law degree courses in India. The first and foremost benefit of appearing in AILET is that it prepares the candidate with the pre-requisite skills and knowledge about the several levels of law courses. Once qualified, the candidate also gets an opportunity to pursue LLB, LLM, or PhD courses from National Law University Delhi, one of the leading Indian law institutes.

Career Scope after Exam

AILET is a discipline-specific entrance examination that grants admission to qualifying students into law degree courses at all levels. Also, since AILET allows access to LLB, LLM, and PhD courses, the career scope is enriched with possibilities in higher studies.
